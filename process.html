Our Design-Build Process

A seamless journey from vision to reality — we guide you every step of the way

1

Initial Meeting

We'll start by meeting with you right on your property, the perfect place to share your ideas, talk through your goals, and begin envisioning what's possible for your outdoor space. Then, we'll follow up with a consultation to ensure we understand your complete vision.

2

Site Analysis & Planning

Our team will carefully evaluate your property's unique features, layout, and opportunities to help shape your future outdoor space. We'll turn your vision into a plan tailored to your lifestyle and backyard while handling all the technical details.

3

3D Virtual Tour

Step into a lifelike preview of your future backyard. Our immersive 3D experience lets you see how everything comes together, so you can feel confident before we build. Make any final adjustments or explore new ideas to perfect the plan.

4

Final Execution

Watch your dream come to life, and enjoy it for years to come. We'll kick off with a pre-construction review, then your project manager and crew will lead the build with expert care and clear communication. All you have to do is watch your dream space come to life.
