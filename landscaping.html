LANDSCAPING FAQS

Got questions about landscape design & construction? We invite you to call and speak to a staff member. Meanwhile, we've put together a few answers to some of our commonly asked questions below.

How long does a landscaping project take?

The timeline for a landscaping project varies based on the scope and complexity. A simple garden installation may take 1-2 weeks, while a complete property transformation with hardscaping, planting, and lighting can take 4-8 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during your consultation and keep you informed throughout the process.

When is the best time to start a landscaping project?

Spring and fall are ideal seasons for landscaping projects in Toronto. Spring allows plants to establish before summer heat, while fall provides ideal conditions for sod, trees, and perennials to develop strong root systems. However, we work year-round and can start planning your project at any time.

How much does landscaping cost?

The cost of landscaping varies based on project scope, materials selected, property conditions, and design complexity. We provide detailed, transparent quotes after our initial consultation and site assessment. We work with clients to create beautiful landscapes that fit their budget without compromising quality.
